Technical Competition Highlights

Accenture UIC Competition: Project A.C.T

AI for Social Good Public Service

We addressed a critical challenge in Singapore: the strain on CDC voucher support caused by high enquiry volumes and the digital divide among seniors. Project A.C.T uses AI-assisted support to handle routine enquiries, prioritising cases for officers to deliver trusted help. This project reinforced the importance of using AI to strengthen public service delivery while preserving human care and accessibility.

Microsoft Competition: AbleConnect

Inclusive Tech AI Personalization

Created AbleConnect, a platform connecting persons with disabilities to jobs that consider their specific needs. It uses AI to recommend roles, generate adaptive resumes, and simulate interviews. For employers, we built a live accessibility checker and dashboards for NGOs to ensure hiring is truly inclusive and data-driven.

SMU Hackathon: Project SLAA

Generative AI SkillsFuture Integration

Developed an AI-driven platform to enhance graduate resumes. The system features an AI chatbot that identifies skills gaps and provides personalized SkillsFuture course recommendations. By integrating Singpass authentication, we ensured a secure and seamless way for Singaporean graduates to utilize their credits and better prepare for their future careers.
