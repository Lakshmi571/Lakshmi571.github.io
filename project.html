Technical Competition Highlights

Accenture UIC Competition: Project A.C.T

AI for Social Good Public Service

We addressed a critical challenge in Singapore: the strain on CDC voucher support. Project A.C.T uses AI-assisted support to handle routine enquiries, prioritizing cases for officers to deliver trusted help while preserving human care and accessibility.

Microsoft Competition: AbleConnect

Inclusive Tech AI Personalization

Created AbleConnect, a platform connecting persons with disabilities to jobs using AI for role recommendations and adaptive resume generation. The initiative was recognized for helping people with different needs thrive in the job market.

SMU Hackathon: Project SLAA

Generative AI SkillsFuture Integration

Developed an AI-driven "Resume Builder" chatbot designed to bridge skills gaps for Singaporean graduates. The project proposes utilizing Generative AI to enhance government initiatives like SkillsFuture through personalized course recommendations.
